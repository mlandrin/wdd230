How is the warmer weather going to affect the drought?

Researchers are concerned that this is going to be a hot summer affecting the amount of water in Lake Powell. The lake is just being to see changes from the Spring run off, but it will not likely be enough to keep the dam in operation. Townmembers are concerned that the water levels will reach "dead pool" levels and they will no longer be able to release water into the canyon.
