Business Owner

The main target audience are business owners in Page Arizona. The average age is 35-45 years old and consist of a variety of businesses. The focus is on entreprenuers and assisting with protecting the integrity of their business. It will function as a network for members to collaborate, work together on projects, and advise one another on marketing strategies.

Concerned Citizen

The Chamber of Commerce is not limited to business owners, and all members of the community are welcome to attend the meetings. Our second user group will consist of concerned members of the community. These people will be between the age of 55-70 and are those who desire to play a role in the growth of the community. They will help provide a unique insight and will use this website to see upcoming meetings and maybe contact a member with questions or concerns.
